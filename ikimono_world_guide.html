えいごクイズ — 生き物のえいごわかるかな？
Q1
All animals must survive in the wild.
What does "survive" mean?
⭕ Correct! Survive = 生きのびる. Great job!
❌ Not quite! Survive means "to stay alive"（生きのびる）.
Q2
Giraffes adapted to reach leaves high in trees.
What does "adapt" mean?
⭕ Correct! Adapt = てきおうする. That's how giraffes got such long necks!
❌ Not quite! Adapt means "to change to fit your environment"（かんきょうに合わせて変わる）.
Q3
A lion is a predator that hunts zebras.
What is a "predator"?
⭕ Correct! A predator = ほ食者. Lions, sharks, and eagles are all predators!
❌ Not quite! A predator is "an animal that hunts other animals"（ほ食者）.
Q4
The octopus uses camouflage to hide from enemies.
What is "camouflage"?
⭕ Correct! Camouflage = ぎ態・保護色. Octopuses can do this in just 0.3 seconds!
❌ Not quite! Camouflage means "blending into the surroundings to hide"（ぎ態）.
Q5
Bees cooperate to protect their hive.
What does "cooperate" mean?
⭕ Correct! Cooperate = 協力する. Bees and ants are amazing at this!
❌ Not quite! Cooperate means "to work together"（協力する）.
